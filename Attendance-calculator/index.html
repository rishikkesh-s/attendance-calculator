How This Calculator Works

  • Total Classes – Total number of classes conducted so far.
  • Attended Classes – How many classes you have attended.
  • Bunkable – The safe number of classes you can miss while staying above the minimum attendance.
  • Attendance % – Your projected percentage if you bunk all possible safe classes.
  • Classes Needed – The number of classes you must attend continuously to reach the minimum requirement.

⚠️ Recommendation: Keep your minimum attendance percentage at ≥ 76% to avoid detention as per most university rules.
